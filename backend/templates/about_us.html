About Us

We are a team of researchers and developers committed to addressing the growing challenge of misinformation online. Our mission is to create tools that improve transparency, restore trust in information, and support the global effort to combat disinformation.

This application was developed as part of MIND's discrepancy detection initiative at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid (UC3M). It is designed to detect discrepancies and knowledge gaps between different language versions of Wikipedia, helping highlight cultural biases and blurred boundaries between factual truth and cultural interpretation.

Our vision is to provide fact-checkers, researchers, and QA systems with more reliable databases and automated tools that streamline the process of validating information. By doing so, we aim to contribute to a more transparent and trustworthy digital information ecosystem.
